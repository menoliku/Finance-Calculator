[APP NAME] ("the App", "we", "us") is an educational stock-analysis and
investment-backtesting tool. This policy explains what data the App collects,
why, and how you can remove it.

## Data we collect

**Account information.** If you create an account, we collect a username,
email address, and password. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt before storage
-- we never store or can retrieve your plaintext password.

**Watchlist.** Stock ticker symbols you choose to save to your watchlist.

**Subscription status.** Whether your account is on the Free or Premium plan.

**We do not collect:** payment card details (not yet -- see "Payments" below),
your real name, phone number, location, contacts, or device identifiers. The
App does not use advertising SDKs or third-party analytics/tracking.

## How we use it

- Account info: to let you log in and identify your account.
- Watchlist: to show your saved stocks back to you.
- Subscription status: to determine which features (analyst ratings, news
  sentiment, price charts, stock comparison, extended recommendations) are
  unlocked for your account.

We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties for
marketing purposes.

## Third-party data

The App fetches public stock market data (prices, fundamentals, news
headlines) from Yahoo Finance to power its calculator, analysis, and
recommendation features. Only the ticker symbol you're looking up is sent to
Yahoo Finance -- no account information, email, or personal data is
transmitted to it.

## Data retention and deletion

Your account data is kept for as long as your account exists. You can
permanently delete your account and all associated data (including your
watchlist) at any time from the app menu: **Menu -> Delete Account**. This
is immediate and irreversible.

## Security

Passwords are hashed (never stored in plain text). Sessions use signed
tokens (JWT) stored locally on your device, not in a server-side cookie
store.

## Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly
collect data from children under 13.
